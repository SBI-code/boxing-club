Who Are We?

Welcome to Float & Sting Boxing, where passion, discipline, and strength come together!

Founded with the goal of empowering individuals through the art of boxing, our club is more than just a gym—it’s a community. Whether you're a beginner looking to get in shape, an amateur fighter honing your skills, or a competitive athlete training for your next bout, we provide the coaching, equipment, and support to help you succeed.

Our experienced trainers focus on technique, endurance, and mindset, ensuring that every member builds confidence, discipline, and resilience both inside and outside the ring. We offer one-on-one training, women’s classes, and a kids' club, making boxing accessible for all ages and skill levels.

At Float & Sting Boxing, we believe that boxing is not just a sport—it’s a way of life. Join us and become part of a family that trains together, supports each other, and fights for greatness!

Ready to step into the ring? Visit us today and start your journey!

Why Join Our Boxing Club?

Expert Coaching for All Levels

Whether you're a beginner stepping into the ring for the first time, an experienced fighter looking to sharpen your skills or just looking for a boxfit class, our trainers are here to guide you. With personalised attention and professional techniques, you'll improve at your own pace.

Build Strength, Confidence, & Discipline

Boxing isn't just about physical fitness; it's a full-body workout that builds mental resilience. Gain strength, improve your endurance, and develop self-confidence while mastering the art of discipline both inside and outside the ring.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our club is equipped with top-quality gear, spacious training areas, and everything you need for a safe and enjoyable experience. We have all the equiptment you could need from boxing gloves to punching bags to sparring rings, we've got what you need to take your training to the next level.

Supportive Community

Join a group of passionate individuals who share your enthusiasm for boxing. Our welcoming and inclusive environment ensures you'll always have encouragement, motivation, and support from teammates and trainers alike.
